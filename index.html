About

Booksite first opened its doors in 1887, founded by the enigmatic wizard bibliophile, Professor Cornelius Everwilde. Legend has it that Professor Everwilde stumbled upon an ancient tome known as the "Book of Whispers" during his travels through the mystical forests of Veridian Hollow. This enchanted book revealed the secrets of crafting a bookstore that would transcend the boundaries of time and reality, allowing readers to immerse themselves in stories beyond their wildest imagination.
